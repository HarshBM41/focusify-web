A Distraction Free Way To
Study from YouTube


Every time you log onto YouTube to look for your videos on your subjects and study,
the homepage provides something more “interesting” and distracts you.

Think of this website as essentially removing the homepage and distracting
“recommendation” videos while still providing the same (optionally rating-sorted) videos
with key functionality of playback speed modifier, full-screen functionality etc.

Also feel free to use the side widgets of a Pomodoro Timer as well as the
To-DO list manager to keep a track of your progress and goals for the session.

Just enter the specific topic you want to study and get started!
(Note: Mini-player video embeds usually don't work for non-educational videos
like music videos or movies, so avoid searching those!)
